As a Product Intern at BrainQ Technologies...

I developed the feature prioritization list for an upcoming Home Medical Device utilizing Impact/Effort matrixes and Use Case diagrams. Primarily, I focused on emphasizing psychological engagement mechanisms to mitigate demotivation risks. BrainQ is a MedTech startup that uses AI to cure neurological disorders, which was selected by Google as 1 of 4 companies in the world expected to transform healthcare with artificial intelligence.

Home Device Presentation

My main internship project was to use market research to develop a vision for what the upcoming at-home iteration of BrainQ's device should look like and present my findings to the company executives. I created a list of proposed features for this product, developing MVP/ideal flowcharts and impact/effort matrixes to determine which ideas to prioritize.

Home Device Use Case

To help communicate the "story" for how the BrainQ Home Device should be used, I created a Use Case timeline conveying what a routine treatment session with this product would look like using Invision Studio.
